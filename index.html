TAP EXERCISE FOR COACHING NOTES
Log every session. Note the weights and which version you did. Over 12 weeks this is your evidence that something is working.
LOG SESSION
Alternate A→B→C continuously. Don't think in calendar weeks — just do the next session in the sequence. Use whichever version fits your day. The sequence never resets.
THE ROTATION
A → B → C → A → B → C → ...

3-MONTH PHASES — TAP TO EXPAND
Load the Pattern
Weeks 1–4
▼
Find your working weights. You should finish sets feeling like you had 1–2 reps left. Log everything — weight, reps, how it felt. This data is what keeps you motivated in month two.
Progressive Overload
Weeks 5–8
▼
Add weight whenever you hit the top of the rep range. This is the only mechanism that forces muscle growth. If the weight isn't going up, check sleep, protein, and effort.
Push the Ceiling
Weeks 9–12
▼
Drop rest to 75s on isolation work. Add slow eccentrics (3–4s down) on key lifts. By now your diary should show clear weight increases vs Week 1 — that's the evidence.
